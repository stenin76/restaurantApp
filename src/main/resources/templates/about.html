Our restaurants

Our restaurants operate in the center of the regional city of Blagoevgrad. Behind our name are teams of enthusiasts who, year after year, realized and upgraded the project of their dreams. It all started in 2012.

What we strive for is good service, innovative and non-standard methods for food preparation, the use of quality food products and special attention to the presentation of each dish.

With the joint efforts of our team, we also implemented this company website, through which food can now reach your home by placing an order from home and having it delivered.
